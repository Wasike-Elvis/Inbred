SERVICES



Wellbred

Wellbred Systems provides a broad range of Architectural Consulting services, Planning Design, Contract Management and Construction Project Management for development projects around Eastern Africa. It has its main offices located on 6th floor of Kenyatta University Plaza, City Square on Haile Sellasie Avenue.

Inquiry

Arch. James Wanyonyi is the partner and the Managing lead consultant for Wellbred Systems. He is an Architect by training. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Architectural Studies. B.A. (Arch. St) from University of Nairobi. He holds a Master’s degree in Architecture (M. Arch). He is a registered Architect with the Board of Registeration of Architects and Quantity Surveyors of Kenya (BORAQS). He is a corporate member of the Architectural Association of Kenya (AAK) and a Corporate member of Uganda Society of Architects (USA). He has over 42 years of experience in the design and management of projects.
