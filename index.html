ABOUT US



WELLBRED SYSTEMS. Company Profile

Wellbred Systems is a partnership firm formed by Inbred Architects Kenya (1981) and Architect –N Systems (2004) to leverage on their technical and management experiences. The firm was registered with the Registrar of Companies on 22nd October,2020 and commenced its practice in 2020.Wellbred Systems provides a broad range of Architectural Consulting services, Planning Design, Contract Management and Construction Project Management for development projects around Eastern Africa. It has its main offices located on 6th floor of Kenyatta University Plaza, City Square on Haile Sellasie Avenue. The Partners, Arch. Christopher Nyongesa and Arch. James Wanyonyi have a vast range of experience and exposure in Kenya, Uganda, South Africa and Somali. They are registered Architects with BORAQ’s. Arch. James Wanyonyi is a corporate member of both AAK Kenya and Uganda Society of Architects (USA). Arch. Christopher Nyongesa is a corporate member of Architectural Association of Kenya MAAK and a member of Institute of Arbitrators Kenya.
